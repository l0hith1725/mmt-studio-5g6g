Note: UE-AMBR is enforced by the (R)AN, not the core. The AMF forwards this value to the gNB as the UEAggregateMaximumBitRate IE in PDU Session Resource Setup (TS 23.501 §5.7.1.6 / §5.7.2.6, TS 38.413 §9.3.1.6). Per-session and per-flow rate limits are enforced at the UPF via Session-AMBR + QER MBR (TS 23.501 §5.7.1.6 / TS 29.244 §5.4).
